## The Parkin Lecture (Prize Lecture of the ESCG)
The Parkin Lecture prize is awarded in honour of the late Dr Andrew Parkin's contributions to the ESCG and his achievements as a scientist and teacher. This Prize lecture seeks to recognise someone for outstanding contributions to promoting or raising public awareness of science, teaching crystallography/science or showing originality in outreach and teaching activities. The lecture is held during the annual ESCG meeting.

## Durward Cruickshank Young Crystallographers’ Prize
The Cruickshank prize was established through a bequest from the estate of Professor Durward Cruickshank. The prize is awarded on an annual basis to a Young Crystallographer (normally with less than 3 years of post doctoral experience) who has made an outstanding contribution in any area of crystallography.

The contribution will be judged on poster and oral presentations and all-round contributions during the BCA Spring Meetings by designated members of the Early Stage Crystallographers Group.

A sum of £250 is associated with this Prize to be used by the recipient to assist them in attending a national or international conference.
