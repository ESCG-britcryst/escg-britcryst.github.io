The 2026 Annual General Meeting of the Early Stage Crystallographer’s Group of the British Crystallographic Association will be held in person at the ESCG Satellite Meeting of the BCA Spring Meeting in Leeds on Monday the 30th of April 2026 from 16:00 - 16:30.

We have a number of positions on the ESCG committee up for election at the meeting. All posts are two-year terms. The current roles that are up for election are as follows:
- Chair
- Secretary/treasurer
- Chemical Crystallography Group (CCG) representative
- Industrial Group (IG) representative
- Physical Crystallography Group (PCG) representative 
- Ordinary Member (one vacancy)

Information for the role of each position can be found under the committtee section on our site, and nominations for these vacant positions can be made through the completion of the online form [here](https://forms.gle/PXA82fzYDzMgbNHGA). We would be grateful if you could forward this information to any contacts who may be interested!
 
Any questions or queries can be directed to the current group secretary Olivia Breen (olivia.breen1@ucdconnect.ie) or current chair Thomas Hitchings (tomhitchings96@gmail.com).
